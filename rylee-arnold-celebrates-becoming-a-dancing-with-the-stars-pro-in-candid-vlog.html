Rylee Arnold Celebrates Becoming a 'Dancing With the Stars' pro in Candid Vlog

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The anticipation is building as the premiere of Season 32 of Dancing With The Stars approaches. With a new cast of celebrities and professionals, the excitement is felt by all. Among the new additions to the pro lineup is the talented Rylee Arnold, who recently shared a heartwarming video that captured the moment she learned about her dream-come-true gig.

Rylee Arnold Celebrates Becoming a Pro

On September 14, Arnold took to her Instagram page to share a heartfelt video that resonated deeply with fans and colleagues. In the heartwarming video, Rylee is seen ecstatically receiving the news of her inclusion as a pro in the upcoming season. She doesn’t waste a second before making a FaceTime call to her older sister Lindsay, who, in a touching moment, guesses the life-changing news.

As Rylee continues to dance around her kitchen, she shares the news with someone else over the phone. Soon after, Lindsay arrives at her sister’s home, and the two share a heartwarming embrace and dance, basking in the glow of this monumental moment.

For Rylee, the journey to becoming a professional on DWTS has been a lifelong dream. She shared with Good Morning America that she had aspired to be on the show since she was just eight years old. Her entire dance career had been a preparation for this moment, and now, it’s finally happening.

As the premiere of Season 32 approaches, Rylee’s excitement is infectious. She eagerly anticipates the experiences that lie ahead. From meeting her dance partner to choreographing routines and participating in iconic-themed weeks.

Lindsay Arnold Also Celebrated Her Younger Sister Becoming a Pro

Lindsay Arnold, who is a Mirrorball champion and a seasoned DWTS pro, took to her Instagram to express her pride and excitement over her younger sister’s journey on the show. She described it as a “full circle moment” and praised Rylee as “the hardest worker, beyond talented, humble, and so kind.”

Lindsay also couldn’t help but express her emotions in the comments of her younger sister’s video, saying, “Crying.” Rylee’s celebrity partner for the season, Harry Jowsey also chimed in with words of admiration. He applauded her as a natural talent and expressed his joy at partnering with someone who is living their dream.

Other professionals from the show, including Britt Stewart, Emma Slater, Daniella Karagach, and Koko Iwasaki showered her with supportive comments.
