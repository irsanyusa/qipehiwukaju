Jennie Garth early life and education

Jennie was raised on a 24-acre horse ranch in the outskirts of Urbana, Illinois with all her six older sisters and brothers. At the time, she dreamed of going to college and owning a dance studio in her adulthood.

Jennie went to Greenway High School for her freshman year. She then transferred to Apollo High School for her sophomore year. At the age of 15 years, she participated in a talent show in her locality where she was spotted by Randy James, who was working as a Hollywood talent scout and manager. Randy encouraged her to pursue an acting career. She, therefore, channelled all her energy into becoming a successful actress.

After being discovered by Randy, Jennie dropped out of high school and moved to Los Angeles, California with her mother. Often, Jennie would receive audition materials from Los Angeles and worked with her coach, Jean Fowler, to enhance her acting skills. She went to auditions in Los Angeles almost daily.
