11 Oscar-Winning Films on Amazon That Will Help You to Get Ready for the Upcoming Awards Season

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Every year, the Oscar ceremony attracts a lot of attention from people all over the world. Audiences around the world choose their favorites and try to guess which film will win the most amount of golden statuettes. And what better way to wait for the next award show than to watch past winners?

1. Forrest Gump

In 1995, Forrest Gump won 6 Oscars, picking up the most important ones: Best Director and Best Picture.

“If you haven’t seen this or if you have seen it, this is so well worth the watch. One of the best movies and best performances by Tom Hanks, it never ages and no matter how many times you see it, it brings tears to your eyes.” — Janet Dunn

2. Interstellar

Even though Interstellar won only 1 Oscar for Best Visual Effects, it was a real hit in 2014.

“A beautiful piece of filmmaking. Thrilling, thought-provoking and highly emotional. The soundtrack of superb quality is the icing on the cake for a truly stunning piece of cinema. The lead and supporting cast all shine. This will go down as one of the greats, in my opinion.” — GL

3. Parasite

Winning 4 out of 6 Oscar nominations in 2019, Parasite attracted huge attention and was undoubtedly the most important film of that award season.

“Honestly, this film is up there with my favorites for how well it’s been made and the care that was taken when considering each shot and sound choice. It’s incredibly well done and a great choice if you’re a fan of a more social realism take on a thriller.” — Taylor

4. Saving Private Ryan

Winner of 5 Oscars out of 11 nominations, Saving Private Ryan is widely regarded as one of the greatest films in history and has etched director Steven Spielberg into the history of world cinema forever.

“I’ve seen this movie before, a long time ago, nevertheless, it still keeps you engrossed, I would most definitely recommend this movie to anyone.” — Les

“I don’t know how many times I’ve seen this film, I’m old enough to remember seeing it first upon its release. It’s still just as good and the acting and production just as brilliant.” — Silver

“I’m no scientist, but I did get the feeling that a significant amount of research went into the writing, and the attention to detail is excellent. Overall, a brilliant watch.” — KimberleighLou

Boasting an Oscar for Best Original Screenplay, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind has been consistently ranked as one of the best love films.
