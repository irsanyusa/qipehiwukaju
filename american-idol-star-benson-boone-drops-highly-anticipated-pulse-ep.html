Breakout pop artist Benson Boone returns with his highly anticipated EP, Pulse, released under Night Street Records/Warner Records. With a collection of five captivating tracks, including the focus track “Little Runaway,” Boone showcases his impressive growth as an artist. Prepare to be immersed in an enchanting musical experience as Pulse takes listeners on a journey through introspection and infectious pop melodies.

Benson Boone Released New EP, ‘Pulse’

Boone has set the stage ablaze with the release of his highly anticipated EP, Pulse. Released under the Night Street Records/Warner Records, this musical offering marks a significant milestone in Boone’s career and promises to leave a lasting impression on listeners.

Pulse showcases Boone’s artistic growth and bold musical direction. Opening with the bouncy and boisterous “Coffee Cake,” listeners are treated to a laid-back breakfast vibe paired with sparkling conversation. The EP continues with the playful pop gem “Lovely Darling,” where Boone’s infectious melodies captivate the audience.

One of the standout tracks on Pulse is the smoldering and angsty “Sugar Sweet,” produced by the acclaimed Jason Evigan. Evigan’s work with artists like Justin Bieber, Dua Lipa, and Maroon Five has earned him recognition and acclaim. With its captivating melody and Boone’s dazzling vocal abilities, “Sugar Sweet” has already amassed over 18 million streams.

Pulse follows Boone’s successful 2022 piano ballad “Before You” and the Walk Me Home EP, featuring hits like “In The Stars,” “ROOM FOR TWO,” and “GHOST TOWN.” The latter marked Boone’s first entry on the Billboard Hot 100 and achieved Top 25 positions on both Top 40 and Hot AC radio.

\

What Changed in Boone’s Writing Process For Pulse?

In a recent interview, Boone sheds light on the changes in his writing process for his latest EP, Pulse. The pop artist reveals that this musical offering felt more natural. It also marked a significant evolution in his approach to songwriting compared to his previous works.

Boone acknowledges that his earlier releases represented the beginning of his music writing journey. However, Pulse stands out as the first body of work where he collaborated with a small group of individuals. This familiarity and comfort played a crucial role in shaping the EP’s creative process.

With Pulse, Boone continues to push boundaries and explore new horizons, solidifying his status as a rising star in the music industry. As audiences embrace his latest musical endeavor, they can expect to witness a further evolution in his artistic journey. Characterized by authenticity, vulnerability, and an unwavering commitment to his craft, Boone showcases his growth and artistic evolution.
