America’s Got Talent Season 13 singer Courtney Hadwin recently took to her TikTok account to share a snippet of new music with her fans. The 18-year-old teased that the song is “coming soon.” This will be Hadwin’s debut single.

Courtney Hadwin Teases New Music for Fans on TikTok

In her new TikTok video, Hadwin shared clips from her audition for AGT back in 2018. The singer was just 13 years old at the time, and she stunned the judges with her rock ‘n’ roll voice. Hadwin made it to the Top 10 of her season.

“But I’m not sorry for being who I am,” Hadwin sings on the new track. “I just hope you’re strong enough to understand / Hurting’s how you learn what it feels like getting burned / And I’m just trying to get back on my feet again.”

Although she still has the same deep, rough tone we heard during her time on AGT, it’s clear that Hadwin’s voice has matured in the years since she appeared on the show.

The clip ends with a newer photo of Hadwin rocking red hair and wearing a leather jacket with plaid pants. The singer wrote “Coming Soon” on the video, so hopefully fans won’t have to wait long to hear the entire song.

This Will Be Hadwin’s Debut Single

What makes this new song even more special is that this is Hadwin’s debut single. In a recent TikTok, she even shared that she just found out the song’s release date. In a previous post, the singer hinted that the track is called “Breakable.”

Hadwin performed covers during her time on AGT, and she even released an EP called The Cover Sessions in 2019, featuring songs by Harry Styles, the Jonas Brothers, and more. In 2020, she released “Happy Xmas (War Is Over),” a John Lennon cover.

Although she has yet to officially release original music, Hadwin has gained an impressive following since her time on AGT. She currently has over 400,000 followers on TikTok and over 500,000 followers on Instagram.
