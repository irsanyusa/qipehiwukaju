Best 'Got Talent' Golden Buzzer Dance Crews

June 2024 · 4 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Trending

Talent Recap

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Group dance acts have been one of the most riveting and appealing to witness during Got Talent competitions. Showing off synchronized movements alongside electrifying stunts, many dance crews have successfully received the coveted Golden Buzzer during their auditions.

We listed some of our favorite Golden Buzzer dance acts on Got Talent. Continue reading to relive each iconic audition.

Best Got Talent Golden Buzzer Dance Crews

1. Mayyas, America’s Got Talent 2022

Coming from Beirut, Lebanon, the Mayyas troupe comprised 36 multi-talented girls from across the country. During their audition, they performed a sophisticated choreography, making shapes using their bodies. Impressed by their act, judge Sofia Vergara pressed the Golden Buzzer, sending them straight to the Live Shows.

2. Murmuration, America’s Got Talent 2023

Murmuration is a dance crew that recently auditioned for AGT season 18. They first graced the stage by executing an intricate dance routine while blindfolded. Such performance convinced judge Howie Mandel to hit the Golden Buzzer, immediately dispatching the group to the Semi-Finals. 

3. Zurcaroh, America’s Got Talent 2018

Founded in 2009, Zurcaroh was an acrobatic dance crew that joined season 13 of AGT. Their audition consisted of twirling and swinging their members along different formations. With their spellbinding performance, host Tyra Banks pressed her Golden Buzzer granting them a free ticket to the Quarterfinals.

4. Ghetto Kids, Britain’s Got Talent 2023

Hailing from Uganda, Ghetto Kids comprised children from an orphanage in the said country. Their audition consisted of a break-dance routine to “Mi Gente” by J Balvin and Willy William & “(Waka Waka) This Time For Africa” by Shakira. New judge Bruno Tonioli pressed his Golden Buzzer in the middle of their performance, the first ever scenario where the buzzer was hit before the end of an act.

5. V. Unbeatable, America’s Got Talent 2019

Originally named Unbeatable in 2012, V.Unbeatable joined AGT season 14 in hopes of finishing as champions. During the Judge Cuts, they performed an acrobatic dance routine – flipping members over the judges’ table and into chairs held high. Amazed by their performance, guest judge Dwyane Wade hit his Golden Buzzer.

6. Boogie Storm, America’s Got Talent: The Champions 2020

Receiving Simon Cowell’s Golden Buzzer back on Britain’s Got Talent season 10, Boogie Storm had high hopes during AGT: The Champions. In the Preliminaries, they performed a dance routine to a medley of “Bang Bang”, “Milkshake”, “Jump”, “Pretty Fly for a White Guy” and “Boogie Wonderland”. After their act, Cowell stole Howie Mandel’s Golden Buzzer, giving the dance crew a spot to the Finals.

7. Light Balance, America’s Got Talent 2017

Light Balance was an impressive light-up dance act back in season 12 of AGT. During their audition, they displayed visual effects through their luminescent wires to Bruno Mars’ “24K Magic” and Deorro’s “Five More Hours.” With this fascinating performance, host Tyra Banks pressed her Golden Buzzer.

8. Entity Allstars, Britain’s Got Talent 2015

Entity Allstars was a dance crew who received judge Alesha Dixon’s Golden Buzzer. During their audition, the group performed to a mix of classical and hip-hop music. They kicked off their dance act in unison until some members of the group did their solo movements.

9. W.A.F.F.L.E. Crew, America’s Got Talent 2020

We Are Family For Life Entertainment Crew was a New York City-Based artist collective that joined season 15 of AGT. Their routine during their audition involved dancing to Chaka Khan’s “Like Sugar.” Wanting to send them straight to the Quarterfinals, Simon Cowell pressed his Golden Buzzer.

10. The Renegades, Canada’s Got Talent 2022

Hailing from Toronto, The Renegades was a dance troupe from the Canadian franchise of Got Talent.  Thrilling the entire audience with their awesome dance act, the crowd themselves pushed the judging panel to give them a Golden Buzzer. Heeding to the call, all four judges agreed to grant them a Group Golden Buzzer.
