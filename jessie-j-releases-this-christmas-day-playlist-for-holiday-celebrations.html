Jessie J released a playlist on Spotify geared toward Holiday listeners. This Christmas Day playlist includes her favorite Christmas music from today’s biggest stars as well as songs of her own.

The singer posted an image of herself wrapped in a sparkling red ensemble as well as a red bow. Many fans believed that she was releasing a new Christmas album right on time for the holidays, but it is in fact a curated playlist by the singer.

There are 24 songs that play for well over an hour. The collection features songs by Mariah Carey, Ariana Grande, Justin Bieber, and Whitney Houston in addition to songs performed by Jessie J.

This is actually a smart way to refresh Jessie J’s Christmas album This Christmas Day. Her first official Christmas album came out in 2018. In a way, this is a re-release, Jessie J’s 2018 renditions of Christmas classics is riddled throughout the playlist.

Jessie J’s Christmas Music Playlist

The marketing and the images of the re-refreshed albums had fans flocking to her page, commenting about how much they love her Christmas album.

This Christmas Day first hit the charts back in 2018. Jessie J enlisted a group of top notch producers including Baby Face as well as David Foster. She also wrote her original “This Christmas Day” single, which appears on the album.

Those who are experiencing grief this upcoming holiday season can turn to “This Christmas Day,” as the song is said to be for those who have lost someone. Many of us can relate to the song if we are looking for music to comfort us this Christmas season.
