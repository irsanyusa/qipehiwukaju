Meet NOIVAS, The Electrifying Singer Making Waves on 'The Voice'

June 2024 · 3 minute read

The popular reality competition series, The Voice, is back with its season 23, featuring a fresh lineup of talented contestants vying for a spot in the top ranks. Among the standout performers is NOIVAS, also known as Savion Wright, an American Idol alum who is ready to make his mark on The Voice stage.

NOIVAS Is an American Idol Alum Competing on The Voice

With his remarkable vocal talent and captivating performances, NOIVAS has captured the attention of both the coaches and the audience, solidifying his position as a standout contestant. He showcased his impressive skills and made it to the Top 30 and Top 24, respectively. Although his American Idol journey didn’t lead to ultimate victory, it laid the foundation for his musical pursuits.

During the blind auditions of The Voice season 23, NOIVAS delivered a show-stopping performance of Sam Cooke’s “A Change Is Gonna Come.” His exceptional vocal range and captivating stage presence earned him a four-chair turn, leaving Kelly Clarkson, Chance The Rapper, Blake Shelton, and Niall Horan in awe.

Beyond his appearances in reality competitions, NOIVAS has been actively pursuing his musical career. He has graced the stage as an opening act for notable artists such as Andy Grammer, Gavin DeGraw, Jason Mraz, and Robin Thicke.

The Voice provides NOIVAS with a platform to showcase his unique style and further explore his musical versatility. With each performance, he aims to captivate not only the coaches but also the millions of viewers watching, leaving an indelible impression with his exceptional talent.

How He Decided to Audition for The Voice

NOIVAS’s journey to The Voice was a result of a personal transformation and newfound inspiration. After taking a hiatus from music for over six years, he found himself drawn back to the art form, thanks to the upcoming arrival of his first daughter.

As NOIVAS embraced his musical journey once again, an email from The Voice casting team arrived in his inbox. They had stumbled upon his old videos on Instagram and were curious if he was still pursuing music.

Initially hesitant, NOIVAS contemplated the idea for weeks, feeling torn between his responsibilities as a father and his dreams. However, after a heartfelt conversation with his partner, he realized that taking a chance on The Voice could lead to an incredible opportunity.

NOIVAS’s leap of faith paid off when he received a response from the producers expressing their admiration for his talent. They extended an invitation to audition for The Voice, marking the beginning of his journey on the show.
