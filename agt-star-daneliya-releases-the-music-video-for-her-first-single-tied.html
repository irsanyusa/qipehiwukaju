'AGT' Star Daneliya Releases The Music Video For Her First Single 'Tied'

July 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Kazakhstan-born singing sensation Daneliya has released the official music video for her deeply personal debut single “Tied.” After rising to fame on reality talent competitions like America’s Got Talent, the now 17-year-old artist is embarking on her musical journey with an authentic, heartfelt song straight from her diary.

Daneliya Drops Music Video For Single “Tied”

The melancholic “Tied” music video showcases Daneliya’s impressive vocal abilities and her vulnerable songwriting. Shot in hazy orange and red tones, the visual follows the singer as she wanders through shadowy rooms with ropes wrapping around her, representing the emotions explored in the lyrics.

In the video’s description, Daneliya shared her excitement for fans to see this real side of herself as an artist. “This is the first track I ever wrote, and now we’ve got a music video to go along with it!” She thanked her video production crew and encouraged listeners to stream the single across all platforms.

The music video was masterfully directed by Yana Malik and Nathan Rideout in collaboration with Cordillera Film. They used light, set design, and movement to heighten the song’s raw feeling. Clips from Daneliya’s performances on AGT were also used, showing the importance of those experiences.

“Tied” marks a new chapter for the singer, who recently decided to go by just her first name. As she explained in an interview with Elle Kazakhstan, she wanted to rebrand herself with a simpler stage name that foreign audiences could pronounce more easily. This also reflects her transition into being a fully-fledged artist instead of just a talented performer.

Fans Praised Daneliya’s Progress in Music

Fans are applauded this more mature musical direction for Daneliya. The evocative vocals and visuals moved many listeners, with one YouTube comment reading: “This song is absolutely sublime.”

The young talent first made waves covering popular songs on competition shows, wowing judges with her vocal control and range. Her cover of Faouzia’s “Tears of Gold” during her AGT run still gives viewers chills. However, her first single “Tied” proves she can craft impactful original work as well.

At just 17 years old, Daneliya clearly has a promising career ahead of her. She brings both stunning vocals as well as vulnerability and truth in her songwriting. The “Tied” music video gives fans a window into the inner workings of this rising pop artist on the verge of stardom.
